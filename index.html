An amazing ceremony on hand. This is a ceremony where Rwandesse and other people around the world come in Rwanda naming gorillas infant. Our special guest was his exelency Paul KAGAME.

2022.08.09(TUE) ~ 09(WEN)

@Musanze, Kinigi, At the base of Rwanda's Volcanoes National Park

Main Program

Intro

Introducing gorilla infants and Kwita Izina ceremony and welcoming special guests.

Kwita

Take time to give name the gorilla's infant from the chosen guest.

Speech

Our speakers take time to thanks people who live near the park to help protect gorillas.

Party

After taking time for the party, where everyone takes time to celebrate. With different artists singing different songs.

Dinner

Over the last editions, the Kwita Izina ceremony has brought together more than 35,000 Rwandans and Friends of Rwanda around the world.
